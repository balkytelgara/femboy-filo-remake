Task E: Doner Shop

In the doner shop, 1 portion of doner costs X tenge, 1 liter of ayran costs Y tenge, and a combo (0.5 portion of doner + 0.5 liters of ayran) costs Z tenge. Daulet needs A portions of doner and B liters of ayran. What is the minimum amount of money he needs to achieve this goal? It's okay if he buys more doners or ayran than needed.

Input Format

A single line containing five integers X, Y, Z, A, B (1 ≤ X, Y, Z ≤ 104, 0 ≤ A, B ≤ 105).

Output Format

Output the answer to the problem.

Examples

Input:
500 800 600 3 2
Output:
2900

Input:
1200 1400 500 3 1
Output:
3000

Solution

Author: @Mazgagzam


x, y, z, a, b = map(int, input().split())

mn = min(a, b)
mx = max(a, b)

answer = min(
    a * x + b * y,
    mn * z * 2 + ((a - b) * x if a > b else (b - a) * y),
    mx * 2 * z
)

print(answer)

Is it efficient enough?

Efficient, passes the tests

Complexity:

Time complexity: O(1)

Explanation:

The solution calculates the minimum amount of money needed to buy the required portions of doner and liters of ayran, considering the cost of individual items and combos.
