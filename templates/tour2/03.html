Task B: Coffee Machine

The strange coffee machine works as follows:

  • A cup of coffee costs X tenge.
  • It only accepts bills with denominations of 500 and 1000 tenge.
  • After buying coffee, the money in the account is immediately reset (no change is given, and you cannot buy multiple cups at once).
  • For example, if the coffee costs 100, then with a 1000 tenge bill, you can only buy one cup of coffee. If there are two 500 tenge bills, then you can buy two cups of coffee.

You have A bills with a denomination of 500 and B bills with a denomination of 1000. What is the maximum number of cups of coffee you can buy with this money?

Input Format

In the first line, three integers A, B, and X (0 ≤ A, B ≤ 10^6, 1 ≤ X ≤ 10^6).

Output Format

Output the answer to the problem.

Examples

Input:
3 2 1200
Output:
2

Input:
2 2 315
Output:
4

Solution

Author: @Mazgagzam


name = input().strip()
surname = input().strip()

s = set()

for a in range(1, len(name) + 1):
    for b in range(1, len(surname) + 1):
        s.add(name[:a] + surname[-b:])

print(len(s))

Is it efficient enough?

Efficient, passes the tests

Complexity:

Time complexity: O(N^2), where N is the length of the names

Explanation:

The solution generates all possible combinations of prefixes of the name and suffixes of the surname and counts the unique combinations.
